Karl Grieshop (PI)

Lecturer (~Assistant Prof.) in Genomics and Bioinformatics at the University of East Anglia. Bachelor’s and Master’s with Mike Polak at the University of Cincinnati (2009, 2012), PhD with Göran Arnqvist and David Berger at Uppsala University (2017), postdocs with Aneil Agrawal at the University of Toronto (2018-2022), and Mike Polak / Josh Benoit at the University of Cincinnati (2023).
